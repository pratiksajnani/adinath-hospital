Our Philosophy: Conservation Before Replacement

At Adinath Hospital, we believe in preserving what nature gave you. Dr. Ashok Sajnani's 35+ years of experience in orthopedic care have shown that many joint problems can be effectively treated without surgery.

Our approach focuses on understanding the root cause of your pain and addressing it with the least invasive method possible. This means faster recovery, less risk, and better long-term outcomes.
